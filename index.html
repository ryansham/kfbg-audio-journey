Why we created this journey

我們為何創作這段旅程

In a city that never stops, KFBG is a place to remember what stillness feels like. These audio journeys were designed by our programme team to guide visitors into a deeper relationship with nature — not through facts, but through presence. Each stop invites you to slow your breath, open your senses, and simply be here.

在這個永不停歇的城市裡，嘉道理農場是讓人重拾寧靜的地方。這些聆聽之旅由我們的節目團隊精心設計，引領訪客與自然建立更深的聯繫——不是透過知識，而是透過當下的存在。每個站點都邀請你放慢呼吸，開放感官，只是靜靜地在此。
